Mechanics: Sample Examples

1. Kinematic Equation

For motion under constant acceleration:

v = u + at

Where:

  • v = final velocity
  • u = initial velocity
  • a = acceleration
  • t = time

2. Newton's Second Law

The net force acting on a particle is:

F = ma

3. Work Done by a Force

The work done by a force F over a displacement d is:

$$ W = \vec{F} \cdot \vec{d} = Fd \cos(\theta) $$
